What are the best features of the bot?

Automatic

Everything is automatic, you don't have to do anything. All features of the bot are activated the instant you add it to the server.

Always on

The bot is always connected, night and day without interruption. You can go to sleep without worry, the bot will protect the server from raids.

Smart

The bot works in a smart way, all its features are applied in a thoughtful way to be the most efficient possible.

Cleaner

The bot works like a dustbuster, except that instead of catching dust, it fights the bad guys that attack your server.

Friendly

A friend seems sad? Or do you want to share with him how happy you are? Give him a cookie, or give him a hug with a command.
